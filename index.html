About Us

Adsto is an athlete management company based out of Mumbai and founded in 12th June, 2017. The founder of Adsto, Mr. Shivanand Lalwani, is a very passion driven sports enthusiast. Adsto visualizes sports from a broad spectrum. The very motive of starting this company was to recognize new and hidden talent and support its growth. ‘Adsto’ by definition means “to stand by” and that’s our motto; to stand by our athletes. We invite every sport and sportsperson with a spark of potential our way and nurture them to fulfil their dreams.

Breaking the norms of the sports world, we recognize every sport and the athlete from a vision of uniqueness. We believe that every sport started as unconventional sport for it to eventually become conventional. Hence, we want to take the onus of building careers out of talent and hard-work and bring more sports to the conventional limelight.

Our Athletes

Yuvyaj Deshmukh

Yuvraj is a 14-year-old dirt biking bravura, who in August became the youngest and the first Indian ever in the history to participate at the World Motocross Championship Juniors in Australia in the category of FIM MX Juniors – 85 CC. Besides being the first Indian to be a part of this prominent championship, Yuvraj also bagged the medal for the youngest participant ever, making the country immensely proud. Yuvraj was supported by Ajmera Racing India at the championship in Horsham, Australia from 24th to 26th of August 2018
